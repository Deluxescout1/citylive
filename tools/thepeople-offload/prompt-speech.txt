the street they exchange ONE short line each. Lines must fit a tiny pixel speech bubble: MAX 32
characters, plain ASCII, UPPERCASE, no emoji. Return JSON:
{ "greet":["..."], "gossip":["..."], "economy":["..."], "politics":["..."], "weather":["..."],
  "work":["..."], "family":["..."], "class_rich":["..."], "class_poor":["..."], "smalltalk":["..."] }

- Exactly 14 lines in EACH of the 10 categories (140 total). All <=32 chars, UPPERCASE, ASCII.
